Push-Pull

This workout focuses more on gaining strength rather than endurance. This workout requires to work out at least 4 days a week, while the fifth one is optional. It is a upper-lower body split workout that focuses more on the uppoer part. However, if your upper body is up to the same level as your lower body and you want to maintain the balance you can change the last 5th day of working out into an ab day / a cardio day / a rest day. Once the workout becomes not challenging enough increase the weights, or add another set to the excercises.

Disclaimer! Make sure you learn the proper technique of the excercises in order to avoid injuries! Even though the workout does not include a warm up routine it helps reduce the chance of injuries, so if you have enought time you should always warm up before starting (however make sure to avoid static streching and do dynamic streches instead).
