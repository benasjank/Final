Push-Pull

A workout made for people that want to and can allow themselvess to train daily and want a variaety of excercises. The workout should be followed for at least 10 weeks. Before starting, make sure to learn proper technique of the excercises to avoid injuries. Unless you already know, on the first day you should do a single max rep workout to figure out what weights you need to use for the workout.

You can click Here to download the program as a pdf file and Here if you want to add it to your activities.
